# How to Change a Disposable Diaper

![Charlize Step 0](http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0007_zpseh0s8sem.jpg)
<p align="center"><i>Note: This tutorial was created with the help of baby Charlize Robert</i></p>

STEP | INSTRUCTIONS | :camera:
:------------: | :------------- | :-------------:
**1** | _Suspicion..._<br /><br />You think you might have a dirty diaper to change. <br />How do you know? | <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0011_zps395qvrsz.jpg" alt="Step 1" height="400" width="400">
**2** | _Initial Observation_<br /><br />Does the diaper appear to be full?<br />Feel the outside of the diaper in this case.<br /><br />If there is a noticeable difference<br />(and you are absolutely sure there is a dirty diaper)<br />Skip to Step 6, otherwise move on to Step 3.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0012_zpsgmfssgul.jpg" alt="Step 2" height="400" width="400">
**3** | _Smell Test_<br /><br />Take a good wiff.<br />Don't be shy!<br /><br />If it's rather unpleasant skip to Step 6,<br />otherwise move on to Step 4.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0019_zpstlkytynp.jpg" alt="Step 3" height="400" width="400">
**4** | _Take a Peek_<br />Still not sure?<br />Let's take a look!<br /><br />Pull the pants/onesie/dress/garment aside.<br /><br />Note: Some diapers have an indicator stripe<br />that will let you know the diaper has been soiled.<br />(e.g. Pampers Swaddlers have a yellow line<br />that turns blue.)<br /><br />Otherwise, you can carefully pull the diaper<br />to the side and see if you notice any<br />discoloration or... *something else*.<br /><br />If so, skip to Step 6, otherwise move on to Step 5.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0020_zpsacstwogc.jpg" alt="Step 4" height="400" width="400">
**5** | _The Step You Should Never Take_<br /><br />Even though this would tell you with absolute<br />certainty that you have a soiled diaper, this step<br />is not recommended.<br /><br />However, the choice is yours.<br /><br />*The Finger Test!*<br />Brave?<br /><br />Let's just assume it's soiled and skip to Step 6.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0022_zpsyzywitiu.jpg" alt="Step 5" height="400" width="400">
**6** | _Collect Your Materials_<br /><br />Make sure you have the following items:<br /><br /><ul><li>Changing pad or table</li><br /><li>Fresh diaper</li><br /><li>Baby Wipes</li><br /><li>The baby (very important)</li></ul>| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0009_zpscrcdjazg.jpg" alt="Step 6" height="400" width="400">
**7** | _Position the Baby_<br /><br />Place the baby on their back on top of the<br />changing table or pad.<br /><br />If using a changing pad choose a safe location<br />to place the pad. The floor is a good place.<br /><br />Note: Sometimes the baby will like to squirm<br />(or roll) so never leave them unattended.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0028_zpsojddphqk.jpg" alt="Step 7" height="400" width="400">
**8** | _Remove Clothing_<br /><br />Depending on what the baby is wearing<br />you'll have to remove part or all of their outfit.<br /><br />Charlize is wearing pants and a onesie underneath.<br /><br />First, we will remove the pants then the onesie.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0032_zpsjighogg8.jpg" alt="Step 8" height="400" width="400">
**9** | _Unfasten Tabs_<br /><br />Unfasten the tabs on the soiled diaper.<br /><br />Don't remove the diaper right away, but check<br />to make sure the baby is not currently<br />going to the bathroom.<br /><br />Note: This is especially important when<br />changing a baby boy!| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0037_zpslqfswbgn.jpg" alt="Step 9" height="400" width="400">
**10** | _Start to Remove the Diaper_<br /><br />If you did receive a *special surprise* (a.k.a. poop),<br />use the front half of the diaper to wipe down your<br />baby's bottom.<br /><br />Fold the dirty diaper underneath the baby with the<br />clean side up by lifting your baby's bottom off<br />the table. You can do this by grabbing both ankles<br />with one hand.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0048_zpsajivslf8.jpg" alt="Step 10" height="400" width="400">
**11** | _Clean Up_<br /><br />Clean your baby's front with a baby wipe.<br /><br />Remember, if the baby is a girl wipe from<br />front to back! This helps keep out bacteria from<br />causing an infection.<br /><br />If the baby pooped, grab another wipe<br />and clean their bum. Also, babies are chunky<br />so don't forget to wipe all the creases in<br />their thighs and surrounding area.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0049_zpsq1mchows.jpg" alt="Step 11" height="400" width="400">
**12** | _The Swap (Part I)_<br /><br />Remove the dirty diaper and set it aside!<br />Move swiftly to prevent accidents!<br /><br />FASTER!| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0052_zpsgmmpsved.jpg" alt="Step 12" height="400" width="400">
**13** | _The Swap (Part II)_<br /><br />Grab the good diaper and place it under the baby.<br />The tabs should be facing the back.<br /><br />Note: To save yourself from this mini two-step panic<br />attack you might want to combine Step 12 and 13<br />by placing the new diaper underneath the baby<br />before removing the soiled diaper. However, I like<br />to live life on the edge so for me it's a<br />two-step process.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0054_zpsa7uqispo.jpg" alt="Step 13" height="400" width="400">
**14** | _Fasten Tabs_<br /><br />Fasten the fresh diaper on both sides with the tabs.<br /><br />Make sure that it is snug enough,<br />but not pinching the baby at any point.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0059_zpsksesynjf.jpg" alt="Step 14" height="400" width="400">
**15** | _Dress Baby_<br /><br />Almost done...<br />Time to dress the baby back up!<br /><br />Since Charlize is wearing a onesie we snap that<br />back on first before putting her pants back on.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0064_zpsyj6zokue.jpg" alt="Step 15" height="400" width="400">
**16** | _Throw Away Soiled Diaper_<br /><br />Dispose of the soiled diaper.<br /><br />A diaper pail is preferred since they<br />are designed to keep out the smell.| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0071_zpshsuqbmnz.jpg" alt="Step 16" height="400" width="400">
**17** | _Squeaky Clean!_<br /><br />You now have a clean and happy baby!| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0075_zpsuatyeps2.jpg" alt="Step 17" height="400" width="400">
**18** | _CONGRATULATIONS!_ :tada:<br /><br />You have successfully changed a diaper.<br />Be proud! :smiley:| <img src="http://i1039.photobucket.com/albums/a471/elizabethsr0813/DSC_0088_zpsvyztwfds.jpg" alt="Step 18" height="400" width="400">
